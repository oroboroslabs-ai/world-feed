WHAT IS WORLD FEED?

World Feed is a sovereign, open-source news and information platform. It replaces X (Twitter) and Hacker News with a single, unified system designed for truth, not engagement.

There is no algorithm optimizing for your attention. There is no corporate curation. There is no shadowbanning. There is no tracking.

You submit. You verify. You decide.

★★★★★ — VERIFIED       Official sources. Multiple independent confirmations. Documentary evidence.
★★★★☆ — RELIABLE       Reputable outlets. Strong sourcing. Corroborated.
★★★☆☆ — PLAUSIBLE      Needs further verification. Some evidence. Developing story.
★★☆☆☆ — UNVERIFIED     Single source. No confirmation. Anonymous reporting.
★☆☆☆☆ — UNRELIABLE     No evidence. Anonymous. Contradicted by known facts.
☆☆☆☆☆ — UNKNOWN        Awaiting verification. New submission. No sources yet.
